World Health Organization App

The team started a series of exploratory teams at the beginning, along with a major effort into building the app that most developers focused on. I lead an exploratory development in Flutter. After 2 days, it was clear that the app I created was the more developed than the main effort. We switched the main effort to it, and I was promoted to a mobile code owner and a Client UI lead.
